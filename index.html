Save My News was a personal, permanent clipping service created by Ben Welsh. It helped journalists and other users preserve more than 20,000 web pages in multiple online archives.

The site was discontinued in 2023 after 'Chief Twit' Elon Musk's management team banned thousands of outside applications reliant on Twitter's infrastructure.

No data has been lost. All the pages archived via this site remain secure in the Internet Archive, Archive.is and WebCite. From its inception Save My News was simply a passthrough to other archives.

If you would like a list of all the URLs you saved here, contact Ben at b@palewi.re.
